Sample Instances for the Risk/Return Summary (RR) 2023 DRAFT Taxonomy

November 7, 2022

1          Goal

The purpose of the sample Inline eXtensible Business Reporting Language (iXBRL) format document (“the Sample”)  is to illustrate the expected use of the 2023 Risk/Return taxonomy for the preparation of technically compliant filings for submission to EDGAR.  It accompanies the Risk/Return Summary (RR) Taxonomy Guide (“the Guide”) for 2023. Users should be familiar with the Guide and with section 6.5.55 of EDGAR Filer Manual volume II (EFM). 

2          Disclaimer

The sample is not normative: nothing in this document nor in the samples supersedes any requirement stated in the Guide, nor the EDGAR Filer Manual (EFM), in the RR or any other taxonomy, nor any other SEC rule or guidance.  In the event of inconsistencies, the rules, filer manual, and guide take precedence.   Text and tables from live filings have been extracted, combined and lightly anonymized and are known to have internal mathematical or date inconsistencies.  The files exist only to help the reader visualize better how the tagging may appear in the context of an entire document, and implies no opinion about the source filings, filer, subject company, filing agent, software vendor, or any other party.  Please direct technical questions to StructuredData@sec.gov.
